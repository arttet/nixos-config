# 🧭 Evolution

This section documents the long-term journey of the NixOS Configuration. It serves as a central hub for understanding how the platform changes over time, why specific architectural paths were chosen, and where the system is headed.

## 🗺️ [Roadmap](./roadmap/)

The Roadmap outlines the strategic direction and upcoming capabilities of the platform. It reflects our current priorities for improving system stability, developer experience, and product features.

## 🏛️ [Architecture Decisions](./adr/)

We use Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) to capture the "why" behind significant design choices. This history prevents the repetition of past mistakes and provides context for new contributors and users.
